# Privacy Policy for syncthingStatus

**Last Updated: November 19, 2025**

## Overview

syncthingStatus is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Data Collection

**syncthingStatus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:
- We do not collect usage statistics
- We do not track user behavior
- We do not use analytics services
- We do not collect crash reports automatically
- We do not share any information with third parties

## Data Storage

All data used by syncthingStatus is stored locally on your device:

- **API Keys**: Stored securely in your macOS Keychain
- **Configuration Preferences**: Stored locally in your user preferences
- **Syncthing Data**: The app only reads data from your local or remote Syncthing instance; it does not store or transmit this data elsewhere

## Network Communication

syncthingStatus only communicates with:
- Your local Syncthing instance (default: localhost:8384)
- Or a remote Syncthing instance if manually configured by you

No other network connections are made. All communication is direct between the app and your Syncthing instance.

## Third-Party Services

syncthingStatus does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Open Source

syncthingStatus is open source software. You can review the complete source code at:
https://github.com/Xpycode/syncthingStatus

This allows you to verify that we honor our privacy commitments.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository:
https://github.com/Xpycode/syncthingStatus/issues

## Your Rights

As syncthingStatus does not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. All app data is stored locally on your device and is under your complete control.
